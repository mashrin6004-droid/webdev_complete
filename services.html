Our Services

We provide a full range of preventive and restorative dental services for all ages. Our team uses modern dental technologies to give accurate diagnoses and comfortable treatment.

Technologies we use

  • Digital X-Rays — High-resolution images with lower radiation for quick, accurate diagnosis.
  • Intraoral Cameras — Detailed views to help explain and plan treatment.
  • Laser Dentistry — Minimally invasive options, faster healing.
  • CAD/CAM — Same-day custom crowns and restorations.
  • 3D Printing — Precise models and surgical guides for improved outcomes.
  • Teledentistry — Virtual consultations from home for triage and followups.

Routine Check-ups & Cleanings

Regular appointments for early detection and prevention. Recommended annually or as advised.

Dental Implants & Restorations

Durable implants and restorations using CAD/CAM and 3D planning.

Orthodontics

Braces and aligners managed by our orthodontic specialist.

Annual Dental Check-Up package

  • Physical examination
  • Cleanings
  • X-Rays (if necessary)
  • Nutritional guidance and hygiene advice
  • Blood tests or laboratory work (if necessary)
